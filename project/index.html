Project Analysis

April, 2013

Client: Truffles and Trifles
Project: Website Redesign
Designer: Rick Swartz
Document: Project Analysis

Site Intent:

The client, Truffles and Trifles, is a 24-year-old cooking school founded with the idea of teaching people how to make the kitchen fun. In short, the client’s website objective is to educate people about the joy of making and eating great food.

The purpose of redesigning the Truffles and Trifles website is to refocus emphasis on the client’s core business as a culinary school. The secondary focus will be to prominently feature their ancillary revenue centers including Catering, Gift Baskets, Fresh Baked Goods, and Gift Certificates. The third and final objective will be to establish a nationwide public awareness with increased website traffic and conversion of the traffic into paying customers.

The Truffles and Trifles homepage is currently devoid of any descriptive copy or imagery whatsoever; and one of the main thrusts of this redesign will be to create a homepage that establishes the client’s brand, and evoke an image of gourmet cuisine for both the more refined, as well as the uncomplicated palate. The client would be well served to consolidate their course offerings into a well defined single page with complete pricing information, along with their catering and event planning services, and gourmet shop to into their own separate pages.

Competitive Analysis:

Based on the competitive research conducted, the client is already well positioned for local search ranking well for Orlando-based culinary searches. However, the demonstrated culinary expertise of Truffles and Trifles also makes them well positioned to promote and capitalize on the sale of their signature products and catering services to the target market.

Target Audience:

The client’s target audience comprises both the residential and commercial markets. The residential audience comprises men, women, and children as well as couples interested in learning about the culinary arts. These people are often referred to as “Foodies”, and include individuals that have highly refined palates to folks who simply love food and beverage and just want to know more about it. To a lesser extent, the residential audience will also have interest in the client’s gourmet shop to include gift baskets, gourmet ingredients, signature products, and catering and event planning services.

The commercial audience presents an excellent opportunity to take a strong position in the catering and event-planning segment of the marketplace, as well as their custom gift baskets as executive gifts. An opportunity may also exist to promote the cooking courses as team building activities.
